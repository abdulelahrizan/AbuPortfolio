My Role in a Team

Leadership

I enjoy team-based projects due to the scale of collaboration involved. I have a deep appreciation for the surprises faced when meeting with the creativity and work ethic of other minds. This led me to realise the importance of keeping my colleagues motivated therefore I tend to take on roles that involve boosting morale by maintaining a positive attitude and diffusing conflicts.

Communication

I make sure to communicate with each team member regarding their experiences to understand how I can best leverage their skills. I find that this helps build a proper picture of the project being managed, and helps spot bottlenecks.

Motivated

I try my best to maintain a pleasant attitude as I understand the importance of the effect it has on those around me. I believe being pleasant and enjoyable to work with takes the bore out of a project and genuinely motivates the team to go the extra mile.
