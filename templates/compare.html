Strengths

As an Ambivert, I realise that I am comfortable with dealing with conflicts while maintaining a pleasant attitude. I enjoy talking to people and building strong relationships while knowing when to remain focused on the task at hand. I enjoy public speaking and do not mind being the center of attention, I own any embarassing moments and am not afraid to politely speak my mind. My ability to stay calm at chaotic and unpredictable moments stems from my high tolerance to risk, thus allowing me to thrive at improvisational decision making. I am an annoyingly curious induvidual who can get really obssessed with the topics of interest. What drives me to learn is limitless amount of opportunities for me to leverage the knowledge gained in the future.

Weaknesses

As much as I can maintain a positive attitude in a group. I occasionally go through neurotic phases that significantly affect my focus and motivation, thus affecting my productivity. I have been learning to priorotize myself during these moments, and been focusing on being appreciative for what I've learned, how far I have come, and for the limitless opportunities available to me. My high tolerance to risk tends to affect my time management skills, Although I am able to thrive in chaotic moments and complete tasks on time, I tend to put myself in them as well which takes a toll on my health.
